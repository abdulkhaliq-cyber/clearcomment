Privacy Policy for ClearComment

Last Updated: November 2024

1. Introduction

ClearComment ("we", "us") provides comment moderation services for Facebook Pages. This policy explains how we handle your data.

2. Data We Collect

To provide our service, we request the following permissions via Facebook Login:

  • pages_manage_posts & pages_read_engagement: Used to read comments on your Page and hide/delete them based on your settings.
  • pages_read_user_content: Used to identify the content of comments for moderation purposes.
  • We store your Page ID and Access Token securely to perform these background tasks. We do not sell your data to third parties.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Fetch comments from your connected Facebook Pages.
  • Analyze text against your moderation rules.
  • Execute "Hide" or "Delete" actions on Facebook via the Graph API.

4. Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at [YOUR EMAIL ADDRESS] or by removing the ClearComment app from your Facebook Business Integrations settings.
